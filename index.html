Mark Holtom

Music Teacher

Mark Holtom has been performing with Christian Orchestras and Bands for over 40 years. He has toured with the Continental Singers as a French Hornist, The New Life Symphony Orchestra from Dallas, Texas as a Trombonist and French Hornist, and Metro Big Band. Mark is currently principal trombonist in the Praise Symphony Orchestra in Santa Ana, band member in Super Kitty and the Side Cats in Whittier, and principal trombonist in the West Coast Big Band in Fullerton. He has performed internationally in France, Italy, Scotland, Poland, Chile, Brazil, and Guatemala. Mark has taught private lessons for the piano, trumpet, French horn, baritone horn, euphonium, and trombone for over 25 years. In addition, he has been an elementary school teacher with the East Whittier City School District for 20 years.
